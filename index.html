Aspiring Front End Developer

Rayna Wiltz is a Petroleum Landman who works with Oil and Gas companies to negotiate business agreements, M&A and mineral right leasing. Rayna is a Registered Professional Landman with over 10 years experience in offshore negotiations and strategic project development. Rayna holds a B.S. in Professional Land and Resource Management from University of Louisiana at Lafayette. Rayna believes that her time spent in oil and gas contract management will be enhanced by the skills she is currently acquiring by being a part of the Moms Can: Code Develop Camp.
